How It Works

  1. Select a post you want to monitor for comments
  2. Set a keyword that will trigger automated DMs
  3. When someone comments with your keyword, they'll receive a direct message
  4. Make sure your Instagram account is set to Professional Account
  5. Messages will be sent from your Instagram account
  6. Use {username} in your response to personalize messages
